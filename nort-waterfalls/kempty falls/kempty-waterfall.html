Jogini waterfall

Kempty Waterfall, near Mussoorie in Uttarakhand, is one of the most popular waterfalls in North India. Falling from a height of about 40 feet, the water creates natural pools where visitors can enjoy a refreshing dip. Surrounded by hills and greenery, it is a favorite picnic and holiday spot for tourists exploring the Queen of Hills.
