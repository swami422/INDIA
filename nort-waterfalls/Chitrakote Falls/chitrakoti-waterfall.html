Chitrakote waterfall

Chitrakote Waterfall, often called the Niagara of India, is the widest waterfall in the country, located in Chhattisgarh. Falling from a height of about 95 feet, the Indravati River creates a spectacular horseshoe-shaped cascade, especially breathtaking during the monsoon season. Surrounded by dense forests, the waterfall glows beautifully under sunlight, and in the evenings, it is often illuminated, adding to its charm.
