Nuranang Falls

Nuranang Falls is a breathtakingly beautiful, high-altitude waterfall in Arunachal Pradesh, India, known for its majestic cascade, surrounding lush greenery, and cultural significance, named after a local girl who aided an Indian soldier during the 1962 Sino-Indian War. The falls originate from the Sela River, creating a two-tiered spectacle that can be enhanced by rainbows on sunny days, offering a serene yet powerful experience for nature lovers and photographers.
