Barehipani Falls

Barehipani Falls is a spectacular, two-tiered waterfall in the Simlipal National Park, Odisha, India, standing as the highest in Odisha and the second-highest in India with a height of 399 meters. The falls are a popular tourist spot, known for their picturesque surroundings of lush greenery and wildlife within the tiger reserve, and are best visited between October and February.
