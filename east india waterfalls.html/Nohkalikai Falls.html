Nohkalikai Falls

Nohkalikai Falls is the highest plunge waterfall in India, located near Cherrapunji in Meghalaya, and is known for its immense height of 340 meters (1,115 feet) and the vibrant green color of its plunge pool. Fed by rainwater, it is surrounded by lush green hills and dense forests, creating a breathtaking natural spectacle. The waterfall is named after a tragic local legend, which adds to its mystique and significance for visitors.
