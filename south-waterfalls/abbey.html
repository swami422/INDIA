Abbey waterfalls

Abbey Falls is a popular waterfall located near Madikeri in the Kodagu district of Karnataka, India, known for its scenic beauty amidst coffee and spice plantations. The falls cascade 70 feet over rocks, surrounded by lush greenery in the Western Ghats. Visitors can enjoy the view from a hanging bridge and experience the powerful roar of the water, especially during the monsoon season when the flow is strongest.
