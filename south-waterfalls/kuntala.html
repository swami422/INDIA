kuntala waterfalls

Kuntala Falls, located in Telangana's Adilabad district, is the state's highest waterfall, plunging 45 to 50 meters down the Kadam River. Nestled within dense forests in the Sahyadri mountain range, it offers a breathtaking natural spectacle, especially during the monsoon season. Visitors can reach the base of the falls by descending about 400 steps, making it a popular trekking and weekend getaway spot, particularly from October to March for pleasant weather.
