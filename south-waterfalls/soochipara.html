soochipara waterfalls

Soochipara Waterfalls, also known as Sentinel Rock Waterfalls, is a three-tiered cascade in Wayanad, Kerala, known for its needle-shaped cliff and surrounding lush forests. Reaching the falls requires a scenic drive through tea estates and a trek along a rocky path, leading to a natural pool ideal for swimming. The 200-meter high waterfall offers opportunities for rock climbing and provides a picturesque setting for nature lovers and adventure seekers to enjoy the local flora and fauna.
