Kamakhya Temple

Kamakhya Temple is a deeply revered Shakti Peetha on Nilachal Hill in Guwahati, Assam, famous for its connection to the goddess Kamakhya, believed to be a powerful menstruating deity. It is a significant pilgrimage site for Tantric and Hindu worshippers, notable for its ancient origins and the symbolic presence of the goddess's yoni within a natural cave. The temple is central to the annual Ambubachi Mela, celebrating the goddess's fertility and regeneration.
