Sun Temple

The Konark Sun Temple is a breathtaking 13th-century UNESCO World Heritage Site in India, architecturally designed as a colossal stone chariot for the Hindu sun god Surya. Known for its exquisite carvings, it stands as the pinnacle of Kalinga architecture, a testament to the power and cultural values of the Ganga Empire, and features intricate details on its massive wheels and horses.
