Vaishno Devi Temple

The Vaishno Devi temple is a significant Hindu pilgrimage site dedicated to Goddess Shakti in the Trikuta hills of Jammu & Kashmir. It's revered as a "Moonh Maangi Muradein Poori Karne Wali Mata" (the Mother who fulfills any wish) and is believed to be one of the most important Shaktipeeths. Pilgrims undertake a challenging trek to reach the holy cave, where the goddess is represented by three natural rock formations called Pindies, and they find a deeply spiritual experience.
