Golden Temple

The Golden Temple (Sri Harmandir Sahib) is a spiritual sanctuary and a symbol of equality and brotherhood, offering a place of peace, devotion, and selfless service for all people, regardless of their background. Its stunning architecture, including its gilded dome, is a marvel, but its true essence lies in its open doors, the communal kitchens (langars) serving free meals, and the serene atmosphere fostered by the sacred pool of nectar, or Amrit Sarovar.
